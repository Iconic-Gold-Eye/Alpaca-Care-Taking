Facial Eczema


Causes

Alpacas are infected with Facial Eczema when they eat Pithomyces chartarum (fungus) as it contains a poison.

Note

Sometimes, sudden death does occur and symptoms may be subtle.

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms.

  • Skin Irritation
  • Crusting and oozing of ears and nose
  • Decreased growth rates in small animals
  • Spontaneous abortions

Treatment

If your alpaca does have Facial Eczema, then give them zinc supplements as this helps fight off the disease.
