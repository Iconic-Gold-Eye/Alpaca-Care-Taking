External Parasites

External Parasites are organisms like lice and ticks.

Causes

External Parasites are caused by a broad range of organisms.

Common Targets

External parasites often affect the following

  • Young Alpacas
  • Immunocompromised Alpacas

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Skin Irritation
  • Disease Transmission

Treatment

External parasites can be treated with chemical treatments.
