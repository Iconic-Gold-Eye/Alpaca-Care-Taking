Mange


Causes

Mange is caused by mites.

Common Targets

Mange often affect the following

  • Alpacas under 2 years old
  • Females who are pregnant
  • Females who are nursing

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Itching
  • Skin Irritation

Treatment

Mange can be treated with Ivermectin, Doramectin, Eprinomectin (Avermectin Class), shampoos, Amitraz, Fluralaner.
