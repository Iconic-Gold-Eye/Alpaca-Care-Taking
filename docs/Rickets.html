Rickets


Causes

Common victims of Rickets are susceptable to the disease due to Vitamin D deficiency

Common Targets

Rickets often affect the following

  • Alpacas under 2 years old
  • Females who are pregnant
  • Females who are nursing

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Hunched Posture
  • Discomfort when moving
  • Legs are splayyed and walking slowly
  • Appears to be leaning backwards when walking

Treatment

Rickets can be treated with Vitamen D injections, but make sure to avoid overdosing.
