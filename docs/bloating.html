Bloating

Bloating is when gas builds up in an alpaca.

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Distended abdomen
  • Discomfort
  • Decreased appetite
  • Increased Respiratory Rate
  • Lethargy
  • Collapse
  • Shock

Treatment

Bloating can be treated by relieving gas, addressing the cause, and supportive care.
