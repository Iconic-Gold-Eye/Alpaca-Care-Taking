Fungal Infections


Causes

Fungal infections are caused by fungi such as ring worm and cause infections like fungal keratitis

Common Targets

Fungi often affect immunocompromised animals

Symptoms

See your veteranarian for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Skin Loss
  • Hair Lesions

Treatment

Fungal infections can be treated with anti-fungal medications, both systemic and topical.
