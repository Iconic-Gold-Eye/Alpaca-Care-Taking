Nutrition

Alpacas are herbivores, primarily gazing on grasses and plants. They have a unique microbial fermentation process in their 4-chambered stomach. Their digestive system allows tough and fibrous plants, but they should not be fed with rich and fatty foods because it could ruin their delicate digestive balance. It is also important to feed them with appropriate supplements for their good health.

Essentials

  • Forage: Grass and Hey, Grasing
  • Water
  • Supplements: Minerals, Calcium, Phosphorus, Potassium, Iron, Magnesium, Manganese, Cobalt, Iodine, and Copper.
