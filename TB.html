Tuberculosis (TB)


Causes

Alpacas are infected with TB when Mycobacterium Tuberculosis is transmitted to an alpaca through the air.

Note

Sometimes, sudden death does occur and symptoms may be subtle.

Symptoms

See your veteranarian immediately for treatment if your camelid has the following symptoms

  • Lethargy
  • Self-isolation from herd
  • Weight loss
  • Coughing

Notice!

TB can be transmitted from Alpacas to humans and vice versa
